SASSRA MEETING MINUTES – SATURDAY 13TH APRIL 2013

 

 

1.     1ST JUNE 2013 – START DATE FOR SASSRA

 

· Members voted for June 2103 for all SASSRA rules and regulations to apply

· Quotes and all payments 1st June and also productions currently filming into June should be re quoted and adjusted accordingly

· all voted in favor

 

2.     STUNT ADJUSTMENTS

 

· members to start informing SASSRA to help start a base rate for stunt adjustments

· members to ask for full information when taking the call for a job

· open communication channels between stunt company and member

· ask stunt companies to compiles their own lists of stunt adjustments, so we can start a guideline for new and existing members

· questions was asked about stunt rehearsals adjustments (to be discussed in the next meeting)

 

3.     10 OR 12 HOUR DAYS

 

· ignore the argument that put for other crew members and international crew (they are paid more money anyway)

· agreed productions will have to improve scheduling

· all voted in favour

 

4.     NO FREE DAYS, 6TH AND 7TH DAY

 

· work for 5 and get paid for 5

· discussion on making the 6th day at this stage normal rate, but 7th day still double time

· all voted in favour

 

5.     NO OVERTIME DEALS ON MEMBER WHO ARE ON WEEKLIES

 

· one stunt company said they run no overtime deals due to that some days you go home early

· all voted against this and no overtime deals will be made

 

6.     JUSTIFICATION OF SASSRA RULES AND REGULATIONS

 

· a discussion was made in reference to issues we have as self employed members.

· Hard to get a bond, finance, most members are not full time etc

 

7.     MEDICAL INJURY LOG FORM

 

·  having a procedure and form on set for SASSRA members.  A protocol that must be followed when injured, forms to be signed by the medic, member and stunt coordinator and a deputy board member on set.

· This will help when issues arrive with claims etc

 

 

8.     SASSRA REPRESENTATIVES

 

· it was agreed that on every production that is running, a deputy for SASSRA will be nominated to liaise on set with production and the board of SASSRA and its members.

 

9.     INVOICES

 

· it was agreed that all SASSRA members create invoices for every job. Even if they are not required to send them into production or the stunt company.

· It will help when pay issues arrives and make it easier to make a case etc

· this will also help the member with getting finance, through proof of income etc

· a SASSRA template will be sent out to all members (both weekly and dailies)

 

10.  PAY SLIPS

 

· it was agreed that stunt companies or productions must provide pay slips with a full breakdown of the payment to the member.

· This can then be crossed checked with your invoice.

· Members must understand that it will take time to get all companies to adhere to this as it is a lot more work for them.

 

11.  CONTRACTS

 

· it was agreed that all contracts should be signed before you work on set

· make sure you read the contract in detail especially the insurance

 

12.  INSURANCE

 

· the insurance policies are being looked into. An example of the insurance policy for Black Sails 2013 will be sent out to all SASSRA members

· There are currently two SASSRA member cases currently running and more news on these will follow.

 

13.  FEATURE RATES

 

· the definition of a 'feature' will be researched and sent out to all members in a newsletter.

 

14.  COMMERCIALS 45 DAY RULE

 

· Inform SASSRA members of the rule that is you are not informed within 45 days from shooting a commercial if you are featured or not then you must be paid your usage fee in full

15.  WEBSITE

 

· all members to put a head shot and other details on the website as soon as possible

 

16.  SPONSORSHIP

 

· any members who have ideas or contacts for sponsorship should contact SASSRA so it can be followed up.

17.  CLOTHING

 

· Chris September is working on clothing for SASSRA and all members will be informed
